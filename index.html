How to Play

1. Buy Elements

Click or drag elements from the shop to your army. All elements cost 3 gold.

2. Build Your Army

Collect up to 7 elements in your army. Higher tier elements are more powerful!

3. Fuse Elements

Drag two elements from your army to the fusion chamber to create more powerful combinations!

4. Battle

At the end of each turn, your army battles an enemy. Build a strong army to survive!
