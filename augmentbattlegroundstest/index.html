How to Play

1. Buy Elements

Use gold to purchase elements from the tavern. Each element has attack, health, and a gold cost.

2. Build Your Board

Click elements in your hand to play them to your board. You can have up to 7 elements on your board.

3. Fuse Elements

Drag two elements to the fusion chamber to combine them into more powerful elements!

4. Battle

At the end of each turn, your army battles an enemy. Build a strong army to survive!
